Conversions

Use this pane to view details of all conversions that are set up in the selected ObjectServer for alerts.status columns, and to set up and maintain conversions.

To view existing conversions for any of the columns, double-click the column name. You can also click the round symbol (on UNIX) or the plus (+) symbol (on Windows) that is shown to the left of the column name.

You can collapse a list of expanded conversions by clicking the round symbol (on UNIX) or the minus (-) symbol (on Windows) that is shown to the left of the column name.
